Welcome to the Supyire Lexicography Project

This project aims to preserve and document the Supyire language through dictionary development, linguistic research, and digital resources accessible to educators, students, and researchers worldwide.

About the Project

The Supyire Lexicography Project focuses on producing dictionaries, databases, and text collections to promote the study and preservation of the Supyire language.

History of Research on Supyire

Research on Supyire dates back to the 1970s and has included phonological, grammatical, and lexical studies led by various linguists.

Current Toolbox Database

The current Toolbox database includes lexical entries, sample texts, and interlinear glossed examples that feed our dictionaries and corpus.
