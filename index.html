Welcome

This project aims to complete the Supyire-French-English dictionaries, a mobile app, and a digital text corpus. Supyire is spoken by over 350,000 people in Mali, and this work will support education, cultural preservation, and research.

About the Project

The Supyire Dictionary and Text Database Project was initiated by Prof. Robert Carlson, with contributions from Robert Sanogo and Michael Otieno. It includes the development of dictionaries, interlinearized texts, and language learning tools for Supyire, a national language in Mali.

Project News & Updates

June 2025 — Dictionary Draft Nears Completion

Our team completed over 4,000 new word entries for the Supyire-French dictionary this month, advancing the project toward its publication goal.

March 2025 — Fieldwork in Sikasso, Mali

Fieldwork sessions were conducted in partnership with local speakers to collect contextual sentences and refine grammatical structures.

January 2025 — New App Development Phase

Development began on the Supyire Dictionary mobile app to make the language accessible globally on Android and iOS.
