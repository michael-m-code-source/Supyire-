Welcome to the Supyire Lexicography Project

The Supyire Lexicography Project is committed to documenting and preserving the Supyire language through dictionary development, linguistic research, and digital tools for education and culture.

About

This project aims to create a comprehensive Supyire dictionary and text database to aid linguists and communities.

History of Research

Research on Supyire has been ongoing since the 1980s, with contributions from dedicated linguists and communities.

Current Toolbox Database

The database stores lexical and grammatical data for digital analysis and publication.
