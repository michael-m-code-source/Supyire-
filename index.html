Welcome to the Supyire Lexicography Project

The project aims to preserve and document the Supyire language through dictionary development, linguistic research, and digital resources accessible to educators, students, and researchers worldwide.

History of Research

Research on Supyire began in the late 1970s under SIL International with Robert Carlson. The team continues to document and modernize the database for Supyire language development.

Current Toolbox Database

The Toolbox database includes lexical entries and interlinear examples used for creating bilingual dictionaries.

Example of a Text

Kòɔ́rɛ̀ nɛ̀ yíí nàgá. — “The man went to the market.”
