Welcome to the Supyire Lexicography Project

This project is dedicated to preserving and documenting the Supyire language through lexicography, digital databases, and linguistic research.

About the Project

Supyire is a language spoken by over 350,000 people in southeastern Mali. The project seeks to publish Supyire‚ÄìFrench and Supyire‚ÄìEnglish dictionaries, both in print and digital form, promoting preservation and literacy.

History of Research

Research began in 1980 under Wycliffe Bible Translators. Over 11,000 words and 400,000 text entries have been collected to date.

Toolbox Database

The lexical database is maintained in SIL Toolbox, soon to migrate to FLEx for dictionary and app publication.
